# KrishiLynk

### Explainable Agricultural Intelligence Platform

KrishiLynk is an AI-driven agricultural intelligence system focused on early crop disease diagnostics, explainable machine learning, and farmer-oriented decision support.

The platform was built to address one of agriculture’s largest global problems:

# Plant Disease Related Crop Loss

Every year, plant diseases are responsible for:

- billions of dollars in agricultural damage
- reduced farmer income
- food supply instability
- excessive pesticide misuse
- large-scale economic inefficiency

Global estimates suggest crop diseases contribute to:

# $220B+ USD Annual Agricultural Losses Worldwide

with developing agricultural economies facing disproportionately higher impact.

---

# The Problem

Traditional crop disease identification methods are often:

- slow
- inaccessible
- dependent on specialists
- difficult for small farmers to access
- lacking interpretability

Most agricultural AI systems also operate as:

## Black-Box Models

where users receive predictions without understanding:

## WHY

the prediction was made.

This creates low trust in AI-assisted agricultural decision systems.

---

# The Solution

KrishiLynk combines:

- crop disease analysis
- explainable AI
- neural attribution systems
- conversational agricultural assistance

to create a more transparent agricultural intelligence workflow.

Instead of only generating predictions, the platform attempts to visually explain:

- which regions influenced the model
- how neural attention behaves
- why certain disease classes were activated

This improves:

- interpretability
- transparency
- trustworthiness
- educational value
- research usability

---

# AI & Machine Learning Techniques

KrishiLynk integrates multiple deep learning and explainability workflows.

## Deep Learning Models

The platform architecture supports:

- ResNet-18
- EfficientNet-B0
- DenseNet-121

for crop disease classification experimentation and comparative inference analysis.

---

# Explainable AI (XAI)

KrishiLynk incorporates multiple explainability techniques including:

| Technique | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Grad-CAM | Visual attention heatmaps |
| LIME | Local prediction interpretation |
| Integrated Gradients | Feature attribution analysis |
| AOPC | Explanation faithfulness evaluation |

These systems help transform AI outputs into more interpretable diagnostic workflows.

---

# KrishiSage

KrishiSage is the conversational agricultural intelligence layer integrated into KrishiLynk.

The assistant is designed to support:

- symptom discussion
- disease reasoning
- irrigation analysis
- nutrient deficiency guidance
- agricultural Q&A workflows

while maintaining concise and practical interactions.
